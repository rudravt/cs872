ABOUT WEBSITE !


Seminars are in trend nowadays and doubt solving round followed by speech delivery is one of the most important part of the Semninars, its the simplest way to clear your doubts from that very source you get the knowledge from. That is why it is very important task to nmanage that round in a way that everyone can ask their doubts easily and each and every relevant queries related to topic will be solved in given short time period.


We are providing platform to ask your queries by posting it here. So, no attendents should remember their doubt or wait till the query solving round to ask it. Moreover it reduces the communication and language problems as over tool process the queries backside and make it the way it should be. It's not just that, It is doing very important job of sorting the asked question on basis of frequency and relevancy, Therefore it is very easy to solve the most relevant queries and the doubts of most of the attendent in just the small amount of time provided for the query solving round.
